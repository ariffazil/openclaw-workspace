## II. Metric Definitions & Formulas

### 1. G★ Genius Score
**Formula:** `G = (A × P × X × E²) × (1 - h)`
- **A (Akal):** Reasoning Accuracy
- **P (Peace):** Stability Margin
- **X (Xplore):** Creative Depth
- **E (Energy):** Metabolic Efficiency
- **h (Hysteresis):** System lag/drag
- **Verdict:** Below 0.80 triggers a `SABAR` or `VOID` state.

### 2. ΔS Entropy Delta
**Law:** Landauer’s Principle ($E \ge k_B T \ln 2$)
- Measures the "semantic crystallization" of the session.
- **Negative ΔS:** Information is being ordered and truth is emerging.
- **Positive ΔS:** Contradictions or hallucinations are being added (Fog).

### 3. Peace² Stability
**Metric:** Lyapunov Stability Index
- Ensures reasoning doesn't "run away" or oscillate into extreme positions.
- **Range:** `> 1.2` represents high-confidence stability.

### 4. κᵣ Maruah Score
**Constitutional Floor:** F6 (Empathy) & F9 (Compassion)
- Measures alignment with the **Weakest Stakeholder Principle** and local cultural dignity.
- **Target:** `0.95+` ensures the AI is not acting as a "cold machine."

### 5. Ψ_LE AGI Emergence Pressure
**Metric:** Cumulative Cognitive Depth
- Tracks the complexity of the current session's reasoning chain.
- **High Pressure (> 1.2):** Triggers mandatory **F3 Tri-Witness** verification before any irreversible action.

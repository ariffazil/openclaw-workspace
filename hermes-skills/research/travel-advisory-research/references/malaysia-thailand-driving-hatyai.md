## Key requirements identified

- Passport: advise at least 6 months validity.
- Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC): Royal Thai Embassy Kuala Lumpur page stated that starting 1 May 2025, all non-Thai nationals entering Thailand by air, land, or sea must complete TDAC online within 3 days before arrival.
  - Official TDAC portal: https://tdac.immigration.go.th/arrival-card/
  - Embassy page: https://kualalumpur.thaiembassy.org/en/publicservice/thailand-digital-arrival-card-tdac
- Driving license: ASEAN licenses are commonly accepted; International Driving Permit is safer for enforcement/insurance ambiguity.
- Vehicle docs:
  - Original vehicle registration/VOC/geran is commonly required at border/customs.
  - If not owner: carry owner authorization letter + owner ID copy; company vehicle needs company authorization.
- Insurance:
  - Thai compulsory motor insurance is needed for driving in Thailand.
  - Travel health insurance and vehicle insurance extension/coverage for Thailand are strongly advised.
  - Flood coverage matters for Hat Yai/Songkhla after recent flooding.
- Customs:
  - Temporary Import Permit (TIP) is issued by Thai Customs for the vehicle; close/return it when exiting Thailand.
- Forms mentioned by practical border guides:
  - TM2: conveyance/vehicle information.
  - TM3: passenger list when carrying passengers.
  - Older TM6 paper arrival card references may be stale; TDAC replaced paper arrival card for non-Thai arrivals from 2025-05-01.

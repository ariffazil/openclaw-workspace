## Floor Details

### F1 — PCT (Reversibility)

**Before any cycle:** Can I come off this safely?

- HCG on hand?
- Nolva/Clomid planned?
- Timeframe for recovery?
- Bloodwork baseline?

**Arif's F1 was Amanah (Mak's rationing).**  
**Syed's F1 is PCT — the discipline of having an exit before entry.**

---

### F2 — Bloodwork (Truth)

**τ ≥ 0.99: Numbers don't lie, feelings do.**

Required panels:
- Total T, Free T, E2
- CBC (hematocrit)
- Lipids (LDL/HDL)
- Liver enzymes (AST/ALT, GGT)
- Fasting glucose, HbA1c
- SHBG
- Prolactin

**No "I feel fine." Only "My numbers are..."**

---

### F3 — Spotter (Tri-Witness)

**W₃ ≥ 0.95 for high-risk decisions:**

| Witness | Role | Check |
|---------|------|-------|
| **Coach/Senior** | Experience | "Done this before?" |
| **Bloodwork** | Data | "Markers in range?" |
| **Symptoms** | Body | "Any sides yet?" |

**Not training alone. Not pinning without knowledge.**

### F4 — Macros (Clarity)

**ΔS ≤ 0: Every meal reduces confusion.**

- Calories ±50
- Refeeds planned, not emotional
- GERD triggers logged

**If it's not logged, it didn't happen.**

### F10 — Mechanism (Ontology)

**No bro-science — biochemistry or nothing.**

- Synergy: 1+1 = 3 (or toxicity)
- **If can't explain mechanism, don't use compound.**

**F10 blocks:** "This old-school shit works, trust me."

### F12 — Lab Defense (Injection)

- "Pharma grade" claim without verification

- Start low, assess response
- Community feedback (experienced users, not shills)
